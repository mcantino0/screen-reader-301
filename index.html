Screen Readers 301

JAWS and Google Workspace

Welcome to Screen Readers 301! In this course, we'll learn about navigating the Google Workspace with JAWS. There are numerous apps included in the Google Workspace, but this course will focus on the apps that are most frequently used in schools:

  • Google Drive
  • Google Docs
  • Google Slides
  • Gmail

Once you learn how to navigate these apps with JAWS, you should be able to apply the same principles to other Google Workspace apps.

To begin the course, just follow the Lessons link in the navigation panel. All the quizzes in the course are optional. If you pass all of the quizzes, you will receive a certificate for 5 CTE hours.
